1. Base apparel agrees to sell particular goods on layby to you, to accept payment
    from you for the layby goods by way of an initial deposit & monthly installment
    and hold the layby goods until purchase price has been paid.
  2. A minimum of deposit of 15% of the purchase price of the layby goods is required when
    entering into the layby agreement.
  3. The purchaser must then make a minimum of one payment per month until the price has fully been paid.
  4. All layby goods purchased in terms of a single layby agreement must fully be paid for and collected
    on the final payment date.
  5. No interest will apply. As such buyer shall have no further recourse against Base Apparel in regulation
    to the layby goods or under the layby agreement.
